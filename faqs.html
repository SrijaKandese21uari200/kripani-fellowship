FAQs

1. What is the Kripani Research Fellowship?

The Kripani Research Fellowship is a one-year research program by Anvi Robotics, designed exclusively for women passionate about Robotics and AI. It provides hands-on research experience, mentorship, financial support, and opportunities to contribute to cutting-edge innovation.

2. Why is this fellowship only for women?

The fellowship aims to bridge the gender gap in robotics and AI research by empowering women technologists with advanced learning, mentorship, and career opportunities in this rapidly growing field.

3. Where can I apply for the fellowship?

You can apply for the Kripani Research Fellowship by clicking the "Apply" button on the website. As part of the selection process, you will need to submit your resume and a video assessment.

4. How are fellows selected, and what opportunities do they receive?

Selection is based on technical expertise, research potential, and passion for AI & robotics through a two-stage process:
Stage 1: Application shortlisting based on resume and video assessment.
Stage 2: Technical interview.

5. What is the time gap between selection rounds?

The time gap varies, but shortlisted candidates are usually notified within a few weeks after each round.

6. If I don’t get selected, are there other opportunities at Anvi Robotics?

Yes! While the fellowship is competitive, Anvi Robotics regularly offers internships, research assistant roles, and other opportunities for skilled candidates.

7. Is the fellowship paid?

Yes, selected candidates will receive a monthly stipend based on their education level:
- B. Tech Final Year Students – ₹25,000
- M. Tech Final Year Students – ₹30,000
- B. Tech Graduates (Freshers) – ₹40,000
- M. Tech Graduates (Freshers) – ₹50,000
- PhD Holders – ₹70,000
- Degree (Relevant Fields) – ₹15,000
