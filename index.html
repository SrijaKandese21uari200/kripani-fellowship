About the Fellowship

The Kripani Research Fellowship opens doors to limitless opportunities for women passionate about robotics and AI.

Opportunities for Kripani Research Fellows

  • Hands-on Experience: Conduct research in robotics and AI technologies.
  • Mentorship: Receive guidance from industry experts.
  • Technology Access: Work with world-class research infrastructure.
  • Learning & Publications: Publish research and present findings globally.

Fellowship Stipend Details

  • B. Tech Final Year Students – ₹25,000 per month
  • M. Tech Final Year Students – ₹30,000 per month
  • B. Tech Graduates (Freshers) – ₹40,000 per month
  • M. Tech Graduates (Freshers) – ₹50,000 per month
  • PhD Holders – ₹70,000 per month
  • Degree (Relevant Fields) – ₹15,000 per month

Eligibility Criteria

Open exclusively to female citizens of India. Final-year students must have approval from their college registrar.
